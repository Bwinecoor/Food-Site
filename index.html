Instructions

First, put the patty on the grill and let it cook for a while. Next, when the patty is almost done cooking put the buns on the grill and let them cook till crunchy. Now, assemble the burger. The order goes as the following, Bun, patty, cheese, pickles, onions, tomatoes, lettuce and the top bun.
